Executive operating artifact

Manufacturing Shape Review

A recurring decision mechanism for customer promise, capability, capacity, control, and leadership depth.

The review sequence
1. Promise

Which customer, safety, quality, delivery, or recovery commitment is at risk or changing?

Define non-negotiables
2. Capability

Which process knowledge, technology, site competency, supplier, utility, or talent dependency matters?

Protect differentiation
3. Capacity

What constraint, utilization, demand, make-buy, capital, and timing evidence changes the configuration?

Compare options
4. Control

Who decides, who executes, what escalates, what is measured, and how does learning return?

Make execution durable

Required decision record

Decision and date

Choice, owner, scope, timing, and review point.

Assumptions and alternatives

Demand, capability, cost, timing, customer impact, and the real options considered.

Evidence and confidence

Baseline, units, source, freshness, uncertainty, and missing proof.

Learning return

What becomes standard, what stays local, and what changes at the next review.

Decision cadence

Weekly

Promise risk, containment, owner, recovery date.

Monthly

Operating economics, root cause, action closure.

Quarterly

Network shape, capital, make-buy, leadership depth.

Annual

Strategy refresh, portfolio, technology, investment sequence.
